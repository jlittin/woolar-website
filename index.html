What We Do

We provide high-value advice and investment services to structured credit portfolios, securitisations, capital relief transactions and fixed income investments.

Our experience spans across buy and sell-sides of the fixed income spectrum in Australia, Asia, Europe and the United States. We have structured, traded and managed portfolios of corporate loans and bonds, residential mortgages, commercial mortgages and other fixed-income assets.

We view this as a future high-growth area within Australia.
